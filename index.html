Good-sees

A "Goodreads" for movies.

The idea for Good-sees stemmed from the long and drawn out conversations you have with someone about movies. First the other person mentions a movie you haven't seen before, and you swear you're going to remember it, and then get home to watch a movie and instantly forget. I also am a huge fan of Goodreads.com, because we all know that it's hard to remember book recommendations. I wanted to use that idea and expand on it, making it more visually pleasing, easier to use, and something that would be more commonly used than Goodreads if it were live. We built it in AngularJs, hitting TheMovieDatabase API, and Facebook for authentication and bringing in friends.

The project is hosted here.
